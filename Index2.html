The Legend Begins

When Zenith of Grissam inherits the Echidna Flame, an ancient sword forged in the war of creation, he's drawn into a battle far beyond his fields. Joined by Lemma of Sciran, and Cathal of Vastuk.

Zenith discovers their weapons are part of the legendary Rysendar—three relics whose united power can end the rising darkness… or unleash it.

"As twisted creatures stalk the land and an ancient god stirs from his prison, alliances will be tested, legends will awaken, and the fate of two kingdoms will rest on the choices of reluctant heroes."

Perfect for fans of high fantasy epics, richly built worlds, and fast-paced quests, The Jade Pandora is the gripping first chapter in a saga of loyalty, legacy, and the unbreakable bonds forged in war.
